Buttermilk

Craving something refreshing in this scorching summer heat? Try this flavoured version of buttermilk which will offer you a blast of fruity flavours. If you are bored of drinking the same old buttermilk, then give it a twist by trying out this flavoured version. Made with strawberries and blueberries, this Berry Buttermilk will be loved by people of all age groups. To sweeten it up a bit, we have added sugar, however, you can add jaggery, stevia or honey to make it healthier. Do try this recipe, rate it and let us know how it turned out to be.

prep time

10 min.

instructions

Step 1

Add curd, strawberry, blueberry, sugar and chia seeds to a blender. Blend to form a smooth mixture. Add 2-4 tbsp water, if the puree is too thick. Blend for some more time to reduce the consistency.

Step 2

Pour the buttermilk into two glasses, garnish with chopped berries and serve.

ingredients

1/2 cup strawberry

1 1/2 cup yoghurt (curd)

1 tablespoon chia seeds

1/4 cup blueberry

2 tablespoon sugar
