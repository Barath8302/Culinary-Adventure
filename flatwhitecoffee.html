Flat White Coffee

Fonds of coffees? Then you need to add this recipe to your list. This basic yet tasty coffee version is perfect to kickstart your day with. You can either enjoy its flavours like this or add some sweetener of your choice. If you don't feel like experimenting much with your cup of coffee but still want that perfect cuppa, then Flat White Coffee is ideal for you. Pair up your cup of coffee with a snack for a wholesome meal. You might have heard about a number of coffee types but flat white is a lesser-known version but still is equally tasty as other coffee variants

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Usually, espresso is made with the help of a coffee machine, but if you don't have it, don't worry. Just add 1 tbsp coffee powder to a mug and add 3 tbsp hot water to it. Whisk well to combine the coffee with the water. Your DIY espresso shot is ready.

step 2

Steam the milk now. You can use a French press to do so or an electric whisker.

step 3

Now gently tilt the mug with the espresso shot and slowly add milk to it in circular motions. Once done, your Flat White is ready to be served.

ingredients

1 tablespoon coffee

3/4 cup milk
